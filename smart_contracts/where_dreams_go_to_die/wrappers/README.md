# Wrapper: Simplifying Contract Integration in our Core Ecosystem

The wrapper serves as a crucial component for integrating our contracts into our core ecosystem. It provides essential functions and data that enable seamless interaction with our module manager contract. Here's how the wrapper enhances our development process:

Facilitating Contract Integration: The wrapper ensures that each contract in our ecosystem can easily integrate with the module manager. This streamlined integration allows us to efficiently manage and coordinate updates and upgrades without relying on upgradeable contracts.

Tracking Existing Implementations: By leveraging the wrapper, we gain visibility into any other implementations of the same contract deployed at different addresses. This information empowers us to make informed decisions and select previous implementations if necessary.

Preserving Immutability and User Choice: The decision to avoid upgradeable contracts brings the advantage of maintaining contract immutability. Additionally, it enables users to exercise their autonomy by selecting the implementation that best suits their needs.

Seamless Version Swapping: Thanks to the wrapper, we can easily swap between different versions of our contracts as required. This flexibility ensures smooth transitions and empowers us to adapt to evolving requirements and advancements in our ecosystem.

Automated Latest Module Retrieval: Utilizing the module manager, builders can effortlessly obtain the most up-to-date implementation of each module. This automation simplifies the development process and ensures that builders are always equipped with the latest features and enhancements.

Enabling Modular Scalability: Our modular system not only facilitates rapid scaling but also paves the way for the creation of a robust cross-chain ecosystem. This modularity empowers us to extend our platform's capabilities across multiple blockchain networks.

By leveraging the wrapper and embracing a modular approach, we are equipped with the tools and flexibility necessary to drive our ecosystem's growth and cater to the evolving needs of our users.
